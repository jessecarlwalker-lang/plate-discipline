Privacy Policy

Overview

Plate Discipline respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Email Subscriptions

When you subscribe, we collect your email address. We use Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) to manage our mailing list. Your email is used only to send you essays and updates from Plate Discipline. We never share your email with third parties for marketing. You can unsubscribe anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

Analytics

We use Fathom Analytics to understand how readers use this site. Fathom is cookieless and GDPR-compliant. It tracks only aggregate page views and basic user behavior. No personal data is collected. No cookies are set. You are never tracked across sites.

Cookies

We do not use cookies on this site.

Data Retention

Email addresses are retained in our mailing list until you unsubscribe. After unsubscription, we retain no personal data. Analytics data is aggregated and cannot identify you individually.

Third-Party Links

This site may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at luke@plate-discipline.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
